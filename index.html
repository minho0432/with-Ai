League of Legends

About the Game

League of Legends is a team-based strategy game where two teams of five powerful champions face off to destroy the other's base. Choose from over 140 champions to make epic plays, secure kills, and take down towers as you battle your way to victory.

Champions

Gameplay

The main objective in League of Legends is to destroy the enemy team's Nexus, which is the heart of their base. To get there, you'll need to work with your team to clear waves of minions, destroy defensive towers, and defeat enemy champions.

For more information, visit the official League of Legends website.
